# Bootstrap<Badge type="feature" text="Improved in 5.0+" />

Freeform includes sample formatting templates built to work with [Bootstrap](https://getbootstrap.com). When using these
templates, Freeform assumes that the required Bootstrap styles and components are already included in your project.
While the templates may not work for all sites out of the box, they provide a solid starting point for building your own.

The following are available:
