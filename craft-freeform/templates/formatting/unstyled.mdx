# Unstyled Grid / Flexbox<Badge type="feature" text="Improved in 5.0+" />

Freeform includes sample, unstyled formatting templates based on [Grid](https://css-tricks.com/snippets/css/complete-guide-grid/) and
[Flexbox](https://css-tricks.com/snippets/css/a-guide-to-flexbox/). When using either of these templates, Freeform assumes that you will provide
all of the necessary styles in your project. While the templates may not work for all sites out of the box, they provide a solid starting
point for building your own.

The following are available:
