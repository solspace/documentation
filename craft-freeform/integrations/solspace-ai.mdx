# SolspaceAI<Badge type="pro" text="Pro" /><Badge type="feature" text="New in 5.15+" />

The **SolspaceAI** integration makes it easy to bring powerful AI capabilities directly into your forms with no extra setup required. [Generate entire forms from a prompt](../forms/builder.mdx#create-forms-with-ai), [analyze and categorize submission data](../forms/fields.mdx#ai), and [reduce spam with intelligent detection](../integrations/ai-spam-analysis.mdx), all from within Freeform.

Built and maintained by [Solspace](https://solspace.com), the team behind _Freeform_, SolspaceAI is a fully turnkey solution that doesn't use any third-party accounts, API keys, or complex configuration needed. You'll get started instantly with **1,000 free credits**, so you can explore its features risk-free. When you're ready to scale, simply purchase additional credit bundles to match your usage.

## Setup Instructions

<VerticalStepWrapper>
<StepMarkdown>

### Enable & Configure the Integration

- Go to the [Integrations](../configuration/settings.mdx#integrations) section in Freeform Settings (**Freeform** → **Integrations**).
- Find _SolspaceAI_ in the list of **service providers**.
- Confirm your email address and site URL or update as necessary.
- Click the **Save** button.

</StepMarkdown>
<StepMarkdown>

### Authorize the Integration

- After the integration is saved, you will see an **Authorize** button appear.
- Click the **Authorize** button.
- If authorized successfully, you'll see a green _Authorized_ status at the top beside the integration name.

</StepMarkdown>
<StepMarkdown>

### Configure the Form

To use this integration within your form(s), you'll need to configure each form individually.

- Visit the form inside the form builder.
- Click on the **Integrations** tab.
- Click on **SolspaceAI** in the list of available integrations.
- On the right side of the page:
  - Enable the integration.

</StepMarkdown>
</VerticalStepWrapper>
