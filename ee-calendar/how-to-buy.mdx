## Updating from EE 6.x

Calendar 5.x for EE7 is a free upgrade for all Calendar 4.x for EE6 customers that purchased licenses on the [EE Add-ons Store](https://expressionengine.com/add-ons/calendar). If you wish to upgrade to EE7, simply ensure that you download a fresh copy of Calendar 5.x from your account and use the files designated for **EE6-7**.

:::info
Anyone with an existing _Calendar_ license purchased from the [EE Add-ons Store](https://expressionengine.com/add-ons/calendar) has automatically been upgraded to access the 5.x version of Calendar.
:::
