## Updating from EE 3.x

Calendar 4.x for EE6 is a free upgrade for all Calendar 3.x for EE4/EE5 customers. If you wish to upgrade from EE4/EE5 to EE6, simply ensure that you download a fresh copy of Calendar 4.x from your account and use the files designated for EE6.
